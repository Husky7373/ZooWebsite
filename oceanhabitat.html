Animals in the Ocean

The Ocean is filled with animals that range in size and shape. Seahorses and Great White Sharks are very different in size, but lize in the same habitat.

Seahorse

Why are they called seahorses? because of the shape of their tiny heads. these animals range from half an inch in size, to 14 inches. They live for around 5 years and feed on even smaller sea animals such as Mysis Shrimp.

Image from Animal Fact Guide

Great White Shark

As soon as the great white shark is born, it swims away from its mother as the mother sees the baby shark only as prey. Young sharks eat fish and rays but as it grows older it will eat much bigger sea mammels like sea lions and seals. Great white sharks have an extremely high sense of smell and if it smells blood, it will go straight to it.
