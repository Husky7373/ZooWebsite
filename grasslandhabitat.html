Animals in the Grassland

Many animals call the grasslands home, including cheetahs and Bison.

Cheetah

Cheetahs are the fastest land animal known to man reaching speeds up to 70 miles per hour. They are meat eating mammals with tan fur covered with circular black spots. These animals can live up to 20 years and weigh around 70-140 pounds.

Image from Live Science

Bison

Bison are herbivores with huge bodys and horns. Although they are large they can still run up to 40 miles per hour, and weigh between 1,000 and 2,000 pounds! So hope you never come accross a wild bison.
